# Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1: Why can't I delete the folder?**  
**A1:** You probably have forms or views inside the folder. You can only
delete empty folders.

**Q2: Can I assign a form to multiple folders?**  
**A2:** No, a form or view can only be assigned to **one folder** at a
time. However, you can move them between folders by removing them from
the current folder and then reassigning them.

**Q3: Does this plugin affect front-end forms or views?**  
**A3:** No, Folders4Gravity is entirely admin-side. It does not alter
any form functionality, entry handling, or front-end display.

**Q4: How do I reorder forms/views within a folder?**  
**A4:** You can **drag and drop** forms/views inside a folder to reorder
them as needed.
